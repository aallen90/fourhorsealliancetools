Gold Farming

Follow these instructions to generate some baller cash while you're sleeping. This applies to your computer. The goal here is to emulate Empire, and setup a macro that automatically collects from the tax collector every 10 minutes.

Setup BlueStacks (installer below), and install Empire through the Play store. Make sure you sign in with your account. Launch Empire, and complete the brief tutorial. Once you hit level 5, go to Settings within the game, and tap "Change User Account". Here's where you type in your credentials. If you haven't saved yours yet, make sure you do. GG does not allow you to port your account to another device until you do so.

Logged in? See your shit? Great! Open up Mouse Controller (installer below)

Within Empire, open up the tax collector screen, and collect if you have not already done so. This is where the screen needs to stay for this method to work. Press "F9" on your keyboard to start recording on MouseController, and click to 10 minute coin gathering option

Wait 10 minutes. Don't move you mouse. Seriously don't fucking touch it. Just wait with the anticipation that you can be lazy as hell from now on. When the timer runs out, click "Collect", then the Ok button (or green check button), and press F9 again.

That wasn't so hard, was it?

Now that you've got it recorded, click "File -> Save", and save the recording somewhere.

Ensure that the "Repeat every" textbox says "0". Click "Start Playback, and make sure that your recording runs correctly.
